Step 1: Watercolours

What's so special about them?

Watercolors come in three different grades: children’s, student grade, and artist grade. The best watercolors for beginners are student grade. Having a good watercolour set really makes a difference in your work.

Step 2: Brushes

Like how a wizard chooses his wand, an artist with their brush

One must always be careful when choosing their brush. Always be open to many varieties at first, after time you'll find your preference. Here are a few ideas on what brushes is of great quality.


-Winsor & Newton Cotman Series 111 Round size 0000 (item number 65749)
-Grumbacher Goldenedge 4620 Round size 000
-Robert Simmons S85 size 5/0 (item number 56403)
-Grumbacher Goldenedge 4620 Round size 0 (item number 06777-1000)
-Grumbacher Goldenedge 4620 Round size 3

Step 3: Watercolour Paper

Why buy it?

Watercolour paper is essentialfor a good experience with watercolours. Only special paper like Watercolour Paper allows the colours to flow beautifully, giving a satisfying effect.
