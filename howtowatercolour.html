Where Do I Begin?

Step 1. Get the right watercolour set.
Watercolors come in three different grades: children’s, student grade, and artist grade. Best watercolors for beginners are student grade. A good example is this Winsor & Newton set, it’s got 24 colors — which means you won’t need to mix your own colors as much. You really just need one good palette, and you’re good to go!
